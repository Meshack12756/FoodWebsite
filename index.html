How To Prepare A Sumptuous Beef Spaghetti

Spaghetti, also known as pasta, is a widely known food across the world, especially among the Italians. People love it due to it's easy preparation, nutritious value and flavour.
It is a perfect food only if prepared the right way.

Today, we'll make some delicious beef spaghetti in a very special way. You will love it. With that in place, Let's jump into it!!

Ingredents

To get started, you will need a couple of Ingredents. This may include:

  • Raw spaghetti
  • Minced meat
  • White Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Elianto cooking Oil(or any of your preference)
  • Corriander
  • Red pepper
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomato paste
  • Seasoning
    • Curry powder
    • Food colour
    • Royco
    • Soy sauce
    • Salt

Procedure

Add water to the cooking vessel and let the water boil. Add salt,then pasta, to the boiling water. Leave the pasta to gently cook for a couple minutes.

To another frying pan, add the Elianto cooking oil and afterwards white onions.Stir till golden brown. Add finely chopped tomatoes to the pan alongside garlic and ginger. Stir and let it simmer.

To a third pan, add another set of chopped white onions and mix it well. Add the minced meat to the mix and stir it well and nicely. Remember to stay alert while cooking. The most credible thing to do to your recipe is to follow it nicely. This is because it maintains the cooking simplicity but taste complexity.

Back to our third pot, the minced meat should have been cooked by around 20 minutes. The aroma should be irresisteble. Add the minced meat to the second frying pan cointaining the tomato mix. Add the tomato paste and stir it really well. Time to spice things up. Make sure you patronage the following steps to make the final outcome tantalizing:

  1. Add the red pepper to the mixture, followed closely by rocyo (or curry).Mix well to obtain a deep yellow mixture.
  2. Place the corriander in the already cooked pasta. This will;
    • Improve the flavour
    • increase the fibre content
    • increase the overall taste of the final dish
  3. Finally, mix the contents of first frying pan with the contents of the second pan. That is, the minced meat stew and the spaghetti. Mix well to an even distribution then finalize the recipe with adding the deep-brown soy sauce. For this step, you can choose to leave the soy sauce unstirred or can still do so. The finall outcome should be a well-cooked, tantalizing and aromating beef-pasta.

Serving Criteria

Based on the quantity of the beef-pasta cooked, you should consider serving with a proper tone. Since the food is a bite size, serving in a bowl is very essential. You should also serve the food with an ice-cold drink like juice, soft drink, eg...coke, or wine. Consider serving the food in the followeing style:

  1. Into a clean bowl, serve the beef-spaghetti using a long handle spaghetti spoon. Ensure the pasta is not full to the brim, just enough to allow room for scooping.
  2. Place the bowl on top of a dinning plate when placing on a table.
  3. Enjoy the meal!! Twirl the pasta with a fork, and fork alone...as you munch down the pasta with elation.
